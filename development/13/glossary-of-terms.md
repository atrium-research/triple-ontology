# Glossary of Terms (Iteration 13)

## CIDOC-CRM Classes

### E7 Activity
**Definition**: An action or group of actions intentionally carried out by one or more actors.
**Ontology**: CIDOC-CRM
**Alignment**: `skos:closeMatch` with `triple:Project`.

### E31 Document
**Definition**: An identifiable immaterial item that makes use of some language and/or symbol system, not necessarily written, to document something.
**Ontology**: CIDOC-CRM
**Alignment**: `skos:exactMatch` with `triple:Document`.

### E90 Symbolic Object
**Definition**: An identifiable immaterial item that makes use of some language and/or symbol system.
**Ontology**: CIDOC-CRM
**Alignment**: `skos:closeMatch` with `triple:MediaObject` and `triple:SemanticArtefact`.

## SSHOC-RO Classes

### SHE1 Dataset
**Definition**: A dataset relevant to the Social Sciences and Humanities.
**Ontology**: SSHOC-RO
**Alignment**: `skos:exactMatch` with `triple:Dataset`.

### SHE3 SSH Project
**Definition**: A research project in the SSH domain.
**Ontology**: SSHOC-RO
**Alignment**: `skos:exactMatch` with `triple:Project`.

### SHE8 Publication
**Definition**: A publication.
**Ontology**: SSHOC-RO
**Alignment**: `skos:exactMatch` with `triple:Document`.
