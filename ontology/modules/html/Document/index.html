1. Introduction

The Document module serves as the core of the TRIPLE Ontology. It defines the `triple:Document` class, representing scholarly publications and research outputs. Crucially, it also acts as the central hub for shared definitions used across the entire ontology, including Identifiers (DataCite), Licenses, Disciplines, and common schema.org properties.
