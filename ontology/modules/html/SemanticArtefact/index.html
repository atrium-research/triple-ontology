1. Introduction

The Semantic Artefact module is dedicated to describing the semantic assets used or indexed by the GoTriple platform, such as ontologies, taxonomies, and thesauri. It extends the MOD (Metadata for Ontology Description) ontology to provide rich metadata about these knowledge organization systems, including their versioning, format, and status.
