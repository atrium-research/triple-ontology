1. Introduction

The Dataset module specializes in representing research data. It defines `triple:Dataset` as a connection point to the DCAT ontology, supporting the description of datasets, their distributions, temporal/spatial coverage, and access rights. This facilitates the discovery and reuse of research data within the SSH domain.

Dataset c back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://gotriple.eu/ontology/triple/Dataset

A collection of data organized for research purposes in the Social Sciences and Humanities domain. A dataset in the GoTriple platform, representing research data with specific metadata including spatial coverage, temporal coverage, and distribution information. It is a subclass of dcat:Dataset. @en
